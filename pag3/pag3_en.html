Characters

Manny

Manny, the good-natured mammoth, is the wise leader of the group, combining his huge stature with a protective nature. Through the challenges of the Ice Age, Manny is challenged not only physically but also emotionally. His journey leads not only to an understanding of community, but also to him overcoming his deepest fears and traumas. With a huge heart and impressive strength, Manny shows that true leadership lies in compassion and caring.

Sid

Sid, the clumsy sloth, brings a unique dynamic to the group with his naive nature and endearing clumsiness. His initial lack of self-confidence is overcome by his adventures and friendships, and he proves to be an essential building block of group harmony. Sid teaches us that sometimes those who are different bring a refreshing perspective and zest for life.

Diego

Diego, the initially suspicious sabre-toothed tiger, develops into a complex character, between his hunting instincts and his loyalty to friends. His transformation reflects that even seemingly insurmountable differences can be bridged by common goals and genuine connections. The combination of keen senses and a warm heart makes Diego a fascinating member of the group.

Scrat

Scrat, the hapless squirrel, is an iconic element of the films. In his relentless pursuit of his acorn, slapstick comedy and mayhem are created. Scrat is not only a comical character, but also symbolises the constant drive for something unattainable, which many viewers can identify with.

Crash and Eddie

Crash and Eddie, the playful possum brothers, add to the humour of the films with their childlike recklessness and adventurous energy. Their chaotic escapades and childish pranks are not only fun, but also a reminder that even in dangerous times, the joy of light-heartedness should not be lost. Her lively manner perfectly complements the ensemble of characters.
